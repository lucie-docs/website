Contribute

How much time do you have?

Less than 10 minutes

  • Talk to your friends and family about online misogyny.
  • Reach out to us on Twitter to let us know what you think.

Less than 30 minutes

  • Post about Opt Out Tools on social media with our Twitter handle @optoutools.
  • If you can code, head over to our start-here repository on Github - look through the open issues for a problem that is small and sweet to fix!

1+ hour

If you can code, head over to our start-here repository on Github and get involved.

We need designers, GDPR advice and many more things so if you think you can contribute and we haven’t listed how here, reach out to us on Twitter.

Donate

We need help. We want to carry on delivering software that solves real-world problems. With your financial support, we can:

  • Make our modeling as accurate and identity-inclusive as possible
  • Make our browser extension multilingual
  • Research how we can better aid reporting abuse to Twitter

OOT is currently a community of around 15 maintainers and a few occasional contributors. We are all working in our spare time making this happen without financial support from external investors. Your money will help us cover our running costs such as online safety training or GDPR and legal advice.
