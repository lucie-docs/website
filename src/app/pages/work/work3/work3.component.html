Our Tools

Browser Extension

Our first tools is a browser extension that works like adBlocker but removes misogyny instead of adverts allowing you to cut trough the noise and focus on healthy debate!

It uses a machine learning algorithm, that is improved and updated by you.

Opt Out will hide any text it considers to be misogynistic. If you don't want to read it, you don't have to. The comment will be automatically reported to the moderators of the social media platform, and sent to our database, so there will be records to support reporting the incident.

We want to lighten the load of emotional labour. Research shows that when handling abuse, victims ask friends and family to help them to screen their social media to protect them from the emotional distress. No one should have to deal with this abuse.

This Website

This website aims to support women getting their voices back.

Here an individual can anonymously submit details of their harassment. This data will be stored, studied and feed the models that our other tools use, for example the browser extension.

We hope to one day show usage statistics of the tools and show women clearly how their submissions are helping to keep their online sisters safe.

Our long-term goal is to display a “virtual” harass map on our website, showing which communities on your selected social media platform are misogynstic or sexually aggressive or just downright abusive, enabling women to navigate the murky waters of online society as best they can.

Activism

We’re holding workshops that give women a chance to meet and share their experiences online and in doing so act in a form of protest.

This helps us identify needed technical infrastructure and ensures that our tools are fit for purpose enabling our tech to be as community-driven as possible.
