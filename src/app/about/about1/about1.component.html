WHO WE ARE.

We are an international collective of coders, data analysts, gender theorists, activists and academics. We began in april 2019, and our team of contributors has been growing since. Together, we are creating tools to help ourselves and our sisters to opt out of sexist hate speech. We want women to have full and equal access to digital spaces so that they can join political debate without fear of being silenced.

Our Vision

To bring an end to violence against woman online

Our mission

To create tools that enable woman to take back control
