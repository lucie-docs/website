WHO WE ARE.

Opt Out is non-profit organisation, founded in 2019. We are an international collective of savvy tech professionals, data analysts, gender theorists, activists and academics. Together we are creating tools to help ourselves and our sisters to opt out from online misogyny. We want women to be able to use the internet to its full potential, as a space where they can join in political debate without fear of being silenced.



Our Vision
We believe in ending violence against women online

Our mission
To create tools that enable woman to take back control
