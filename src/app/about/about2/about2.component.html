OUR CORE VALUES

Empowerment

We build safe spaces online through automatic reporting of detected misogyny. Our focus isn't on victimization, but accountability.

Inclusivity

All woman face misogyny in some form. We actively encourage participation from minority groups as this helps improve our models.

Open Source

Our tech is open-source and as community-driven as it can be. We believe in growth by our community, not just for.

Privacy

When it comes to your data, we believe in transparency. We never do anything with your data you don't consent to.
