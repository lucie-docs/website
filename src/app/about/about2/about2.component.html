OUR CORE VALUES

Empowerment

We encourage reporting of abuse and promote a safe space to share experiences; our focus isn't on victimization, but instead on communication and support. We provide the tools to enable and empower ourselves and each other.

Inclusivity

All woman face misogyny in some form. Unfortunately, there is a data gap when it comes to misogyny against certain groups such as queer woman and woman of colour. We actively encourage participation form minority groups as this helps train our models.

Open Source

Experience sharing and community support is vital to our activism, as is starting dialogs between us. Our tech is open-source, and we believe in promoting growth trough knowledge and skill sharing.

Privacy

When it comes to your data, we believe in transparency. We see ourselves as extension of the GDPR; our models are taught trough crowd sourced data, given to us freely through active participation.
